Coronavirus Daily News Update

Monday, March 27, 2023

Note: As the COVID pandemic enters a new phase with the end of the nation’s public health emergency in early May, Sarasota Memorial has stopped posting daily updates to the Coronavirus News Update webpage as of Friday, March 24, 2023. SMH will continue to track the number of patients hospitalized for COVID and other clinical indicators that help monitor community spread. If SMH detects signs of a future surge or begins to experience capacity concerns due to COVID, we will resume sharing important community updates on smh.com/news.
